Facilitation Resource

Gendered
Community
Policing Toolkit

A facilitation resource for building trust, dialogue, and gender-responsive approaches to community safety.

This toolkit accompanies ICAN's animation on Gendered Community Policing and supports facilitators working on inclusive security, peacebuilding, police reform, and community dialogue.

Facilitator Checklist

Check off items as you prepare for your session.

Session Readiness 0 / 9 complete
✓
Assign one or two note-takers to record key points, discussions, and next steps.
✓
Check that you have a working laptop, projector, or screen with sound for the ICAN animation.
✓
Ensure a stable internet connection if the animation is streamed.
✓
Print copies of exercises, discussion questions, and worksheets such as stakeholder maps or lists of barriers and solutions.
✓
Gather pens, markers, and sticky notes for group and reflection activities.
✓
Arrange seating to support open discussion and eye contact among participants.
✓
Create a welcoming space with clear ground rules for listening and respectful dialogue.
✓
Bring any additional readings, visuals, or case studies on gendered community policing.
✓
Prepare a plan for follow-up, such as a debrief, another meeting, or joint action steps.
